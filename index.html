Welcome to My Baking Page!

Hi! My name is Riley, I'm 10 years old, and I love to bake. I love baking all kinds of things…bread, pie, cake…but cookies are my favorite!

I started baking because it's fun, and it's way better than cleaning my room. Plus, when you're done baking, you get to eat something delicious. That's a pretty awesome reward!

What I Like to Bake

  • 🍪 Cookies (the best!)
  • 🍞 Bread
  • 🥧 Pies
  • 🍰 Cakes
  • 🥐 Whatever else sounds yummy!

Why Baking Is Awesome

Baking is like doing a science experiment that you can eat. You mix ingredients together, put them in the oven, and something amazing comes out. Sometimes recipes don't work perfectly, but that's part of learning.

My favorite part is when the whole house smells like fresh cookies.

My Cookie Mission

My goal is to bake lots of different kinds of cookies and keep getting better every time. Maybe one day I'll even invent my own famous cookie recipe!

Thanks for visiting my baking page. I hope it makes you hungry... and maybe inspires you to bake something too!
